NewCRU is prototyping wine experiences in the mobile space. If you knew the secret ingredients in some wines, you'd know why they're secret.

Co-founded by Barry Seifer and John Manoogian III

New Cru spans the gap between fans of natural, sustainably-grown products and the world's artisan wine growers & producers.

Find what you like, wherever you are. Free, using your smartphone. Personal, intuitive, fast, informative, and geographically relevant.

New Cru intends to change what the wine conversation is about, who controls the conversation, and where the conversations happens, adding a new voice: Yours.

Forget the hype. Enjoy the journey. Drink what you like.
